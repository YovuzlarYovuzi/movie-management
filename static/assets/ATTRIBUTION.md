## Battle Game Assets

This project includes a small subset of assets downloaded from the Phaser 3 examples repository:

- Source: `https://github.com/photonstorm/phaser3-examples`
- Paths used: `public/assets/animations/*`, `public/assets/skies/*`, `public/assets/particles/*`
- License: MIT (see upstream repository)

If you replace these placeholders with your own anime-style packs, keep the same filenames or update `static/js/scenes/BootScene.js`.
